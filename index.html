About Us

Since 1999 in Natick, Ma., our goal at Happy Dog Daycare has been to give dogs a safe place to make friends, run, play, or just nap in the sun....in other words a home away from home. There is a large fenced in yard surrounded by woods and wildlife, and during the summer pools both large and small are set in the shade of large maples. In the winter, its a yard to make doggie angels and just roll around and enjoy the wonder of it all.

Services

Happy Dog is a home site pet daycare with indoor/outdoor areas for the dogs to enjoy. The yards are fenced and have grass, not stone or mulch.

The area is large and functional for lots of play and exercise. With woods bordering the yard, there are sightings of wildlife....later giving the dogs fun dreams about chasing the bunnies and wild turkeys they have seen.

We are a crate free daycare.

We have on-site grooming service upon request without the use of crates - which means less stress for them and you.

Dog training an integral part of Happy Dog daycare, as we utilize the daily situations that come up to help each dog achieve good individual manners and proper behavior in groups. We offer other training as needed.

We have pick-up and take-home service for Happy Dogs. More information about this service is available during interviews.

We Care

Lauren and Barbara are life-long dog lovers, and between them have seven dogs. Lauren has 4 black labs, ranging in age for 12 years to 14 weeks, and Barbara lives with 3 corgis ages 7, 6, and 2. Between them they have had 12 dogs over the years, and have learned that just like children, each dog is unique in his/her own way. They love what they do, and they love each dog they have the pleasure to care for.

Both Lauren and Barbara have a love for the natural and subscribe to a holistic and organic style as much as possible in the food, medical care, and everyday basics. They believe that keeping it as simple as possible is best for all the dogs.

Routine is also important to dogs, providing them with security and a sense of comfort. Lauren and Barbara cater to those "creatures of habit" by offering a daily schedule of activity and rest, with an occaisional diversion thrown in, and always, there is a consistent amount of individual attention and belly rubs.
